About me:

My name is Suju Sapkota and I am a senior at The University of Nebraska Omaha. I was born in Germany but I am from Nepal. We moved to The Land of Opportunity when I was eight, so I was raised in Nebraska.

When I turned 18, I immediately bought a ticket to go to Australia on my own during the summer. It was the best summer and one my favorite experiences so far. Last summer, I took a trip to Nepal. This summer, I visited Colorado and Chicago with my friends. My family and I went on a east coast trip to drop of my brother at Brown University. We visited Virginia, Ohio, Delware, New York, Conneticut, and Rhode Island.

I also sing and dance. I don't believe I am the best but I love having fun on stage. During Nepalese festivals, I participate to perserve my culture. I also choreograph dances when I have time. Choreographing cultural dances not only reminds me who I am and where I am from, but it also teaches the dancer about your values and tradition.
